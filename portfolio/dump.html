Typography

The standard for print materials was to use our serif font, Adobe Garamond, as a primary reading font, while our sans-serif font, Univers, was reserved fir ornamental use. Univers has a ton of weights and varieties, many of which appeard regularly in print materials. Often narrow and thin fonts were used in print
