My role

Design lead // visual + interaction design // content strategy // brand development // user research

Timeline

February 2014 — today

The Challenge

Located in one of the fastest growing regions in the United States, Texas State University is expanding rapidly, with year-over-year enrollment increases for nearly the last two decades. Keeping up with this growth from a technological perspective has been a major challenge, resulting in a few predictable problems:

  1. Over 400 websites are now published inside our CMS, making content strategy and site governance haphazard and difficult to scale.
  2. Brand guidelines and design methodologies remain dependent on print mediums, and have failed to reach digital audiences.
  3. New devices and user expectations for websites require vigilant and nuanced ways of communicating.
  4. Evolving standards for accessibility require our team to adopt complex, unfamiliar skill sets.
