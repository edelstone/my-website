Quick bio

I’m Michael Edelstone, a digital designer comfortable with both the product and marketing sides of the craft. Currently I work with the extremely capable team at BrickFTP. Before that I led design and user experience for Texas State University.

I’m into bicycles, movies, astronomy, national parks, and adventures with my dog, Chloe. One day I hope to be recruited by an intergalactic team of fighter pilots like Alex Rogan in The Last Starfighter.
