My story

I’m Michael Edelstone, a digital designer focusing on utilitarian products and brands. Currently, I’m part of the extremely capable team at Kuali (we're hiring). Before that, I managed design initiatives at BrickFTP and Texas State University.

I enjoy bicycles, movies, astronomy, national parks, and adventures with my dog, Chloe. One day I hope to be recruited by an intergalactic team of fighter pilots like Alex Rogan in The Last Starfighter.

Some things I know pretty darn well

  • HTML: Scratch-built, readable, and standards-compliant markup. We’re essentially BFFs.
  • CSS: Roger that. Big fan of animations and flexbox, with real-world experience writing vanilla, Sass, and postCSS.
  • Accessibility: Universal design is central to my process. I’ve shipped interfaces conforming to the strictest standards.
  • Product UX: Research, sketches, app copy, and clickable prototypes using a refined, understandable process.
  • Design tools: Figma and Chrome DevTools are my current favorites, but I enjoy Sketch and InVision too.
  • Git and GitHub: From React apps to static sites, I open source, GitFlow, and the command line.
